How To Play

Game Setup
The game is played by rolling two dice (later rounds may use three dice).
Players earn or lose points based on the dice results.
Scoring Rules (Two Dice)
Exact Sums
If the total is 5, gain +1 point.
If the total is 10, gain +2 points.
Medium Sums
If the total is greater than 5 and less than 10 (6–9), lose −1 point.
High Sums
If the total is greater than 10 (11–12), lose −2 points.
Low Sums
If the total is less than 5 (2–4), no points are gained or lost.
Doubles (Special Rule)
If both dice show the same number, gain points equal to that number.
Examples:
2 & 2 → +2 points
6 & 6 → +6 points
Doubles override all other rules.
Three Dice Mode
When using three dice, the same rules apply: Scoring is based on the total sum. If all dice show the same number, the player gains points equal to that number.
Example: 4, 4, 4 → +4 points
